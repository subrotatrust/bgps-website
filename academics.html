What We Teach

Our Core Subjects

Following the latest Ministry of Primary and Mass Education guidelines, we focus on balanced psycho-physical development.

Bangla

Developing foundational literacy, creative writing, and appreciation for our rich cultural heritage.

English

Focusing on four basic skills: Listening, Speaking, Reading, and Writing through CLT methods.

Mathematics

Building logical reasoning and problem-solving skills starting from basic arithmetic to geometry.

Science

Elementary Science topics that encourage children to observe and explore the world around them.

New Assessment Policy

Under the new national curriculum implemented in 2026, we have shifted from traditional high-stakes exams to Continuous Assessment.

  • No formal examinations up to Class 3.
  • Evaluation based on class projects, presentations, and behavior.
  • Focus on "Learning by Doing" and soft skills development.
