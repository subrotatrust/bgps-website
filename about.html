About Our School

A tradition of excellence in education since 1990.

Our Journey

Rooted in Knowledge, Growing in Character

Berail Government Primary School has been a pillar of the community for over 50 years.

Since our founding in 1990, we have evolved from a small village school into a modern primary institution. We focus not only on the National Curriculum but also on digital literacy, sports, and cultural development.

Our Vision
